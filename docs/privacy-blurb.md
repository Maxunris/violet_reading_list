# Privacy Blurb

Violet Reading List stores your saved links, folders, tags, and preferences inside your browser's extension storage. When browser sync storage is available, your data may sync through your browser account; otherwise it stays in local extension storage on your device.

The extension does not require an external account, does not run its own backend, and does not sell your data. Metadata such as page title, description, hostname, and favicon is collected only to display saved entries in a readable form.

Users can export and import their library as JSON at any time.
